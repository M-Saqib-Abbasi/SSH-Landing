Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our CoreSuit and services.

What is CoreSuit?

+

CoreSuit is our flagship enterprise software suite that includes modules for financial management, inventory, POS, travel management, and more. It's a comprehensive solution designed to streamline all aspects of your business operations.

Can I use only specific modules?

+

Absolutely! CoreSuit is modular by design. You can start with just the modules you need (like General Ledger or POS) and add others as your business grows. All modules integrate seamlessly with each other.

How does implementation work?

+

Our implementation process includes discovery, configuration, data migration, training, and go-live support. We assign a dedicated project manager to ensure smooth deployment tailored to your business needs.

Do you offer custom development?

+

Yes, we specialize in custom modules and extensions to meet unique business requirements. Our "On Demand Modules" service lets you get exactly the functionality you need.

What ongoing support do you provide?

+

We offer 24/7 technical support, regular software updates, security patches, and performance optimization. Our support packages are flexible to match your business requirements.
